Special Considerations for Religious Educational Path

Primary Text Study

  • Systematic study of the Four Books: Analects, Mencius, Great Learning, Doctrine of the Mean
  • Gradual introduction to the Five Classics: I Ching, Book of Documents, Book of Poetry, etc.
  • Dedicate 30-60 minutes weekly to primary text reading

Ritual Practice Development

  • Identify local Confucian temples or communities for practical experience
  • Consider short-term visits to China, Taiwan, or Korea to observe authentic practices
  • Document and reflect on ritual observations

Teaching Portfolio Development

  • Begin creating teaching materials from Phase 3 onward
  • Develop visual aids, discussion questions, and lesson plans
  • Record practice teaching sessions for self-improvement

Community Engagement

  • Connect with religious communities to understand educational needs
  • Volunteer teaching opportunities to gain practical experience
  • Build relationships with established teachers for mentorship
